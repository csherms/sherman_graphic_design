PRICING

I charge a base fee of $250 (USD) to create an initial mockup for a logo design. This first draft will include a watermark that will be removed upon full purchase. The base fee is non-refundable and includes up to 2 revisions of the mockup. The full price for the finalized logo is an additional $300 (USD), totaling $550 (USD).

Upon payment of the full amount, the watermark will be removed, and the logo will be delivered in the required image formats (e.g., PNG, JPEG, or SVG), either via email or in person depending on the client's location. Once the unwatermarked logo has been purchased and delivered, it becomes the client's property and is non-refundable.

The typical timeframe for delivering the initial mockup is 1 week, but this may vary depending on the complexity of the design. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee if faster turnaround is needed. Any additional revisions or changes beyond the initial 2 revisions will be billed at a rate of $50 (USD) per hour. These revisions are in addition to the base fee of $250 (USD).
