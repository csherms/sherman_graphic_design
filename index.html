PRICING

I charge a base fee of $250 (USD) to do an initial mockup for a logo design. This first draft design will come with a watermark that will be removed upon full purchase.

The full price of any logo is an additional $300 (USD) on top of the initial $250 for the mockup (totaling $550 USD). Upon payment the watermark will be removed, and the logo will be delivered in any required format via email or in person depending on the client's location.

Logo design timeframes may vary depending on the complexity of the design. The typical length of time for an initial mockup is 1 week. All revision work and design edits beyond the additional mockup price are billed hourly at a rate of $20 (USD) per hour in addition to the flat rate fee of $550 (USD).
