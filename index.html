PRICING

I charge a base fee of $200 (USD) to do an initial mockup for a logo design. This first draft design will come with a watermark that will be removed upon full purchase.

The full price of any logo is an aditional $300 (USD) on top of the initial $200 for the mockup (totalling $500 USD). The watermark will be removed and the logo delivered in any required format via email or in person depending on client location.

Logo design timeframes may vary depending on the complexity of the design. The typical length of time for an initial mockup is 1 week. All revision work and design edits beyond the additional mockup price are billed hourly. The revision rate is $20 (USD) per hour in addition to the flat rate fee of $500 (USD).
